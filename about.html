About

Meadow & Stack is a small software development company run by Neil Davidson. He recently (Summer 2021) stopped working in data analysis research for Edinburgh Napier University to set up this business in North Uist.

The company is devoted to developing technology solutions in collaboration with rural businesses and services. Recent projects include prototyping an interface for Planet Heat, a heating startup in Renfrewshire and developing a community engagement app for Rural Housing Scotland.
